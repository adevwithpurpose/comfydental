- **Smile Makeovers & Veneers**

- **Root Canals**
- **Teeth Whitening**
- **Digital X-Rays & 3D Scans**

## Patient Journey (Process)

1. **Visit One: Consultation**
    - Digital X-Rays & 3D Scan
    - Comprehensive Exam & Smile Assessment
    - Treatment Plan & Fee Proposal
2. **Visit Two: Treatment Prep**
    - Preparation of Tooth Structure
    - Deep Cleaning / Scaling & Impressions
    - Comfort Management
3. **Visit Three: Final Results**
    - Final Placement & Bonding
    - Bite Adjustment & Polishing
    - Smile Reveal

## Service Timelines
